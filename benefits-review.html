Workplace Performance Review

Employee Benefits Data Capture

A structured capture of your existing pension and benefits arrangements. Used by the Finch Theory team to identify inefficiencies, opportunities, and where the value sits before any recommendations are made.

You do not need to know every answer precisely. "Don't know" is a useful answer in its own right. Documents can be attached at the end. Most people complete this in around twenty minutes.

What happens next. Once submitted, you receive a confirmation email. Matthew and the Finch Theory team review your submission and any attached documents before your next meeting. Information is held confidentially and used only for the purposes of the Review.

01Organisation details

Basic organisation and contact information.

What you do and who for. A sentence is fine.
02Current pension provision

Workplace pension scheme details. Ranges and approximate figures are fine where exact numbers are not to hand.

For example: NEST, Aviva, Standard Life Master Trust, Royal London GPP.

05Supporting documents

Documents are sent by email rather than uploaded here. The button below opens a pre-addressed email so you can attach files from your machine and send them directly.

Documents that help us most

Attach whatever you have. Common items:

  • Pension contribution schedule or monthly upload
  • Member-level scheme data (gender, date of birth, job title, salary)
  • Benefits and renewal documents
  • Any provider statements, scheme literature, or policy paperwork
✉ Submit documents by email

Opens a pre-addressed email in your default email program. Attach files from your machine, then send. Use the same address as your form contact email where possible so we can match the submissions.

Optional. Anything we should know about the files, missing data, password-protected documents, or items still being gathered.
06Additional notes and priorities

Anything not captured above. Priorities, concerns, or specific outcomes you are looking for.
