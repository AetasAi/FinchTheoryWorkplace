Methodology

How the calculator works

Four cost categories, each estimated using widely accepted research figures. These are sector averages, your specific number will differ. The Workplace Performance Review finds the real one.

01

Retention cost

Number of employees × turnover rate × (75% of average salary). Replacing a mid-level employee typically costs 50% to 200% of annual salary in recruitment, onboarding, and productivity loss during transition.

02

Productivity loss

Approximately 25% of UK employees report financial worries affecting their performance, costing roughly three hours of focused output per week per affected employee.

03

Absence cost

UK average sickness absence is 9.4 days per employee. Roughly 20% of that absence is linked to stress and financial pressure. Daily salary cost × stress-linked absence × workforce size.

04

Benefits waste

Average UK employer spends roughly £800 per employee per year on benefits. Around 15% of that is typically unused or poorly understood, a clear inefficiency in most pension and benefits arrangements.
