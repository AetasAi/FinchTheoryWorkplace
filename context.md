## What it does

Identifies and fixes the hidden commercial cost of financial pressure in the workforce — through three pillars:

1. **Clarity** — Benefits and pension review; employees understand what is already in place
2. **Confidence** — Group workshops and one-to-one financial education sessions
3. **Stability** — Retained ongoing support and partner services

Entry point is always the **Workplace Performance Review** — free, four stages, no commitment until proposal.

SMEs and LLPs with 10–250 employees where financial pressure in the workforce is showing up as reduced productivity, higher absence, unexplained disengagement, or staff leaving earlier than they should.
