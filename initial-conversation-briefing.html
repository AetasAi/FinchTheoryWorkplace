Initial Conversation Briefing

A short briefing before our first conversation. It helps us arrive prepared, focus the time on what matters to you, and avoid covering ground that is already obvious. Most people complete it in around ten minutes.

What happens next. Once submitted, you receive a confirmation email with a copy of your responses. Matthew will review them before your Initial Conversation and bring relevant context to the call. Nothing here is shared beyond the Finch Theory team.
