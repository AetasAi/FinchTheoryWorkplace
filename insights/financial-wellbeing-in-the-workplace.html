Building Stronger Teams

In today’s fast-moving business landscape, employee wellbeing is much more than a nice-to-have benefit. For small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), financial stress is one of the leading causes of distraction, disengagement and absence at work. Yet many organisations invest in wellbeing initiatives without a clear strategy, leaving both employees and leaders unsure of real impact.

That’s where Finch Theory comes in. We provide a practical, structured approach designed to help organisations support their people more effectively, enhance engagement and build stronger, more resilient teams.

Why Financial Wellbeing Matters

Financial wellbeing has become a core driver of performance. When team members are worried about money, it affects focus, productivity and even retention. Employers can no longer treat financial wellbeing as an optional add-on. Instead, it must be part of a thoughtful strategy that delivers real value for people and the business.

The Finch Theory webinar unpacks this challenge. It looks at why traditional benefits often miss the mark, and introduces a simple, scalable framework that goes beyond apps and tick-box solutions.

What You Will Gain

Participants will walk away with practical insights and tools, including:

  • A clear understanding of why financial wellbeing should be part of your organisational strategy
  • Deeper insight into where employee benefits often fail to deliver value
  • A structured, scalable approach to financial wellbeing that supports diverse employee needs
  • How Finch Theory can strengthen your organisation and your people

This isn’t just theory, it’s practical guidance aimed at leaders who care about culture, performance and long-term success.

Who Should Attend

This webinar is ideal for SME leaders and anyone responsible for people, performance or culture, including:

  • Business owners and founders
  • HR and people managers
  • Finance directors and CFOs
  • Operations and team leaders
